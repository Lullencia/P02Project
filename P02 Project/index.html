Basic Branding

What does the company do, or offer?

The company is one that specializes in selling guitar and related equipment such as amplifiers, tuners, cables, and more.


Who is the company to potential customers?

To potential customers, the company comes off as approachable, yet reliable. It doesn't make beginners feel inadequate, but openly welcomes and nurtures their path to learning the guitar. For intermediate to advanced players, the company also serves as a home and place of comfort while still being knowledgeable and factual.


Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

The primary draw of the company's products is the company's image as trustworthy and reliable. This will be achieved through the type of content displayed such as in-depth breakdowns and details described on product pages and the overall look and feel of the website. Another avenue would be through customer reviews and feedback.


Brand Components

Font

General Sans

Color Palette



The majority of the website is intended to be fairly neutral to accomodate for the variety of products and brands promoted. However, red will be used as the primary accent color.

Potential Art Direction

Generally, photographs of guitarists will be used for non-product pictures. Photographs with warm tones and shorter depth of fields are preferred. Black and white photographs are fine as well. Candid photographs are preferred in natural musical environments such as stages, recording studios, etc.



Product images will be professionally shot photographs against light/white backgrounds to demonstrate tangibility and authenticity.




Icons will have similar features to the ones pictures. Intended have slightly rounded corners to insinuate friendliness and trustworthiness. Icons will also have a solid and simplistic style.
