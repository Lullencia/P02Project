Description

Michael Clifford and Gibson have collaborated over the course of a few world tours to develop his signature model guitar. The Michael Clifford Melody Maker rocks a double cutaway Melody Maker design for rock solid playability as well as a classic, cool look. The body is crafted of swamp ash for its resonant tonal characteristics and moderate weight. The rounded, maple neck profile provides incredible rigidity and sustain while imparting a smooth and comfortable feel. It features a black Richlite fingerboard and signature red acrylic "X" inlays for fast and effortless playability. The BurstBucker 3 Humbucker delivers a muscular PAF style voice in full humbucking mode, and the push-pull volume control coil splits the humbucker for a brighter, single coil voice. In addition to the tone control, the Kill Switch Toggle offers further tonal options and effects. This one humbucker guitar is no one trick pony! The distinctive Jet Black Cherry finish completes Michael's vision of his signature guitar. The Michael Clifford Melody Maker is the perfect guitar for the player starting his first gig to rocking out stadiums and everything in between.
